# Session lesson: philosophy-led proposal structure and collaboration-field patterns

Use this when a public HTML proposal for an AI sales/operations system needs to carry the user's higher-level philosophy, not just implementation mechanics or UI screens.

## What happened

A proposal iteration had already shown multi-platform discovery, private-domain/IM management, AI/human collaboration, and a command-center UI. The user then corrected two conceptual issues:

1. Do not reduce the collaboration model to a fixed “small group” mechanism. A small group is only one possible operating form; the report should explore broader message/collaboration structures.
2. The proposal should foreground the two confirmed philosophies: **全 AI 自动化** and **自举分形**.

The fix was to add a philosophy section and collaboration-forms section rather than only tweaking the workbench UI.

## Durable pattern

When the user is developing a strategic/product thesis, do not let the report collapse into a feature list. Raise stable philosophical concepts into the early narrative, then let sections and visuals instantiate them.

Good structure:

```text
Hero thesis
  -> Core judgment
  -> Two operating philosophies
  -> Target audiences / channels
  -> Automation route
  -> Full-chain system map
  -> Collaboration forms
  -> Product-like workbench sample
  -> Commercialization / productization path
```

For this class of AI sales-ops proposal, two useful philosophy framings are:

- **全 AI 自动化 / operation philosophy**: not “AI pretends to be human” and not “no humans involved.” It means every repeatable, structured, replayable, auditable information-processing step defaults to AI; humans move upward to trust, judgment, negotiation, relationship, and critical close.
- **自举分形 / product philosophy**: the system first sells the product, then real sales activity trains the system, and the trained system becomes a product. The same pattern repeats at different scales: one conversation becomes FAQ, one lead flow becomes an Agent, one partner path becomes a channel network, one internal tool becomes an external product.

## Collaboration-field patterns

When the raw concept mentions a “small group,” abstract it as a **可控协作场域** and list multiple possible operating forms. This keeps the idea product-like and avoids locking the strategy to one tactic.

Useful forms:

- **单客协作舱**: one high-value customer/partner gets a dedicated operating space; AI handles knowledge, notes, and reminders; humans handle trust and key decisions.
- **伙伴推进室**: agents/service providers get materials, talk tracks, cases, FAQs, and follow-up rhythm so they can copy the sales motion.
- **共创训练营**: high-agency individuals are filtered through tasks, feedback quality, and sustained action.
- **场景实验室**: vertical themes such as e-commerce客服高峰,售后压力, or channel conversion become places to test scripts, materials, and product boundaries.
- **IM 聚合收件箱**: distributed conversations across WeChat, Feishu, QQ, Telegram, enterprise IM, communities, and platform inboxes are summarized, reminded, archived, and escalated without forcing every user into one channel.
- **影子助手模式**: for high-cognition prospects, AI may stay behind the scenes and provide drafts, risk flags, and next-action advice to the human operator instead of speaking directly.

## Public-safety wording

Avoid over-specific or risk-heavy wording in external artifacts:

- Avoid presenting “small group” as the only mechanism. Prefer “可控协作场域,” “伙伴推进空间,” “协作舱,” or “IM 聚合工作台.”
- Avoid “多级分销.” Prefer “伙伴网络,” “转介绍,” “渠道递推,” or “渠道协作.”
- Avoid personal backstory or first-person rationale. Keep the idea as a product/operations pattern.

## Visual implications

For HTML/PDF deliverables:

- Use a two-card philosophy section for 全 AI 自动化 and 自举分形.
- Add a loop visual for self-bootstrapping/fractal logic: 销售 AI 客服 → 记录真实运营动作 → 沉淀为系统模块 → 形成销售运营产品.
- Use a grid of collaboration forms rather than one mock chat group.
- If the workbench includes a collaboration space, label it generically (e.g. 可控协作场域 / 伙伴推进空间) and show AI/human boundaries.

## Validation checks

Before delivering, verify:

- the proposal's top narrative includes the philosophical concepts if the user has identified them as core;
- collaboration is not reduced to one tactic;
- public wording avoids personal/private backstory and sensitive sales hierarchy language;
- the product UI sample still shows module boundaries and AI/human guardrails;
- static/PDF-first hygiene remains intact: no anchors/images/scripts/motion unless explicitly approved.
